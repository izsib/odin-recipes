Soup :)


Description

yum, Soup! Everyone loves soup, right? It's a great way to warm up and a perfect dish to eat on a cold day."

Ingredients

  • can of soup, your choice
  • toppings (optional)

Steps

  1. open soup can and add to a pan
  2. turn on stove to low
  3. wait until soup bubbles
  4. add to a bowl, turn off stove
  5. add toppings, if using
  6. wait for soup to cool
  7. enjoy
