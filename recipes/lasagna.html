Lasagna :)


Description

yum, lasagna! from simply recipes: "Everyone loves a good lasagna, right? It's a great way to feed a crowd and a perfect dish to bring to a potluck. It freezes well. It reheats well. Leftovers will keep you happy for days."

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese

Steps

  1. pre heat the oven
  2. grease a 9 x 13 pan
  3. lay noodles
  4. layer tomato sauce
  5. layer cheese
  6. repeat until pan is full or ingredients depleted
  7. bake for an hour or so
